I did the tutorial for Django in a weekend and then almost immediately set about working on a full-fledged website. Following tips from one of my supervisors at my Dining Commons job, I designed and developed an employee scheduling website that was intended to replace their current one. As it happened, that plan fell through because, unbeknowest to me, Dining Services (the organization that encompassed Dining Commons) was already in negotiations with another company and was planning on installing their product. Nonetheless, my Django skills and my general web development skills improved greatly over the three months that I worked on the website.

The site can be viewed here: Dining Commons Demo. Note: website-breaking bugs may be encountered because I do not maintain it. You have been warned.
